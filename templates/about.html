About Galen

Photo Credit: Daniel Weld

Galen Weld is a undergraduate student at Cornell University's department of Computer Science. An avid photographer, Galen has been taking photos since he was 8 years old. Originally from Seattle, Galen enjoys shooting landscapes and nature images, as well as wilderness photography. When he's not holding a camera, Galen can be found ice and rock climbing, mountaineering, skiing, or riding his bike.

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact Galen at galen.weld@gmail.com.
